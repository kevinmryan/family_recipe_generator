* `2-4` cucumber, diced
* `2-4` tomato, diced
* `1 bag` frozen corn, rince until thawed
* `1 bunch` basil, chopped
* `½` lemon's worth of juice
* `to taste` salt and pepper

> **Ingredients**

---

> Combine all ingredients and toss until mixed. Add black beans (rinsed) if desired.
